    100% platinum liquid silicone:
    The use of platinum liquid silicone provides optimal thermal resistance, stability and flexibility. The range of Silikomart silicone products withstand temperatures between -60¬∞C and +230¬∞C, making them safe for use in the oven, microwave, dishwasher, fridge and freezer. Silikomart silicone moulds are ultra-flexible, feature a good grip and are resistant to cracks and aging. Thanks to the silicone injection process, these moulds allow for greater details and a longer lifespan, with a durability guaranteed for up to 3,000 uses. Platinum liquid silicone is completely non-toxic, odourless and tasteless: it does not add or absorb any flavours from food ingredients. The material flexibility and the glossy surface ensure an easy release from the mould.

    • Care:
    It is recommended to clean the mould thoroughly before use.
    To increase the lifespan of your Silikomart silicone moulds, do not use strong detergents.
    However, if white marks appear after several times in the dishwasher, immerse the mould in boiling water for a few minutes.

    Quality and safety:
    Silikomart moulds are produced with raw materials that are compliant with domestic and international standards.
    Silikomart is dedicated to meeting quality controls to ensure customers have reliable products which comply with all standards.
