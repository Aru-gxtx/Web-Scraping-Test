Silikomart Silicone Mold for Ice Cream Pops, Classic Shape

Make your own ice cream at home on a stick with this kit,
which comes with a clear plastic tray 15-5/8" x 11-5/8" x 1/2" high.

The silicone mold is 1 inch high and made of two halves,
each with 6 oblong ice-cream-portion cavities.

Each ice cream cavity
has a volume of 3.0 Oz
and measures 1-7/8" x 3-5/8" x 1" high.

Each ice cream cavity
has a slot through which to slip a wooden stick
(50 included, each one measuring 3/8" x 4-1/2").

Silicone temperature resistance -76F to 446F
